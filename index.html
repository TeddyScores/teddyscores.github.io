About

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Teddy Teske. I am a film composer, sound designer, and re-recording mixer with over a decade of professional experience in film, trailers, and AV marketing. My career began in Los Angeles working on small independent films, which led to credits on major studio and big-budget productions.

In addition to custom music and sound design for film and trailers, my passion is to create cinematic SFX libraries and music assets drawn from original material developed for films — including unreleased sounds that never made the final cut. Every project is approached collaboratively, working one-on-one with directors and producers to help shape sound that serves the story. Whether it's a custom design or a score for your next trailer campaign, I am here to help tell your story.
